Biography [CV]

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at GT, supervised by Prof. Zhigang Peng. Recently, I got my Ph.D degree at The University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Prof. Xinming Wu and cooperated with Prof. Zefeng Li . Before that, I received the M.Sc degree of Geophysics at CUGB in 2020 and B. Eng degree of Geophysics at China University of Geosciences in 2017.

My research lies at Geophysical application using Graph Neural Network(GNN), Foundation Model and Convolutional Neural Network(CNN).
